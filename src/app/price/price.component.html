Logo Design Process
Step 1. LOGO DETAIL BRIEF

Fill out this logo detail form please. It helps us to understand your activity and your needs. Based on your responses we will create a list of objectives that need to be met by the logo design.

Step 2. PAYMENT 50%

You can pay 50% using PayPal or you can hire us on Freelancer.com or Upwork. After that we start working on next step.

Step 3. RESEARCH & DEVELOP

We need some time to research your activity and generate our ideas on paper. Researching is very important part of any design process. We will study your activity and your competitors. Only after good researching we can design a logo that represents the uniqueness of the business or product. Based on the design brief and research conducted, we sketch ideas and then experimented with them on the special software.

Step 4. PRESENTATION & EDIT

When we ready to present several concepts, we will send you document which display the logo designs created with explain our decisions. For concept that you'll like, you have unlimited revisions until you will satisfy your needs.

Step 5. PAYMENT & DELIVERY

Once you are happy with the final design logo, you can pay last 50%, we will prepare files for use on web and print. I will also prepare a document with all logo configuration and file with vector format.

Step 6. SUPPORT

We provide any technical support after the completion of the project.
